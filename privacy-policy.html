#Privacy Policy Last Updated: March 10, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how the Budget SMS Assistant (“the Service”) collects, uses, and protects information when users interact with the Service via text message. Information We Collect When you use the Service, we may collect the following information: Your phone number Text messages sent to the Service Budget categories and spending amounts you submit Basic usage data such as message timestamps This information is collected solely to operate the budgeting functionality. #How We Use Information We use the collected information to: Process budgeting commands sent by users Track spending totals within user-defined budget categories Respond with updated budget balances Improve functionality of the Service We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. #SMS Communications By texting the Service phone number, you consent to receive automated responses related to your budgeting activity. Message frequency varies based on user interaction. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier. #Opt-Out You may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying: STOP After receiving a STOP request, the Service will cease sending messages to your phone number. #Help For assistance, reply: HELP You may also contact the Service operator for support. #Data Security Reasonable technical and administrative measures are used to protect the information collected through the Service. However, no system can guarantee absolute security. #Data Retention Information is retained only as long as necessary to provide budgeting functionality or improve the Service. #Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.
